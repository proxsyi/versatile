Embers

  • Drop or upload any photo (drag a file anywhere on the page).
  • It auto-traces the outline from the image's edges.
  • All tools stack: Auto-trace, Points (click an outline that snaps to edges), Draw (freehand), and Erase all combine. undoes your last step.
  • Ignite — a spark throws a storm of embers that swarm in and draw the outline in fire.
  • Rain mode: ignite with rain on and the outline is painted by falling drops as each one strikes it.
  • In the fire: drag to stir, tap to spark, C to calm.
