Embers

  • Drop or upload any photo (or drag a file anywhere on the page).
  • Embers auto-traces the outline from the image's edges.
  • Refine it: Points to click an outline that snaps to real edges, Draw to sketch freehand, Erase to clean up, or the Detail / Density sliders.
  • Ignite — a spark throws a storm of embers that swarm in and draw your outline in fire.
  • In the fire: drag to stir wind, tap to throw a spark, toggle Rain, press C to calm.
Keys: U upload · E edit · R rain · M mute · C calm · ? help
